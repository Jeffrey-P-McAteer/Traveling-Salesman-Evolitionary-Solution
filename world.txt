According to http://www.math.uwaterloo.ca/tsp/world/
the world.tsp record is a path of length 7,512,218,268
discovered by Concorde TSP code on June 5, 2007, using
CPLEX as a linear-programming solver.

The bound shows that Keld Helsgaun's tour has length at
most 0.0474% greater than the length of an optimal tour.
